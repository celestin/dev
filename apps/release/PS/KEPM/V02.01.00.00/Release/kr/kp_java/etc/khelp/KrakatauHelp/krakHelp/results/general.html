General Navigation

There are two main aspects to the Metric Results Viewer:

  1. The Symbol List - located on the left hand side of the Interface.
  2. The Result Viewer - located to the right of the Symbol List, this is where the results (values, histograms etc) are shown.

You may also navigate in the Metric Result Viewer using the Project/File/... tabs to the top left of the screen, by clicking on the appropriate tab the corresponding list of symbols will be shown.
