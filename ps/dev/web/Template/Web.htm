Home page

This is the place to start. A home page can be considered to be a combination of a business card and a corporate brochure.

It is a very cost-effective way of projecting your desired image.

If we look for benefits then there are no reprinting or postage costs with a home page and it will never arrive all crumpled up having spent weeks in the post. It can be viewed by anyone, anywhere and presents a consistent, high quality image. Also it can be updated or changed whenever required.

A home page is also very expandable. Web technology lets you have as many chapters to your home page as you like. This can include your services, products, customers and general information. Great use can be made of pictures and images.

The main point is that this page is how the world sees you and must reflect the image and business you want to project.
