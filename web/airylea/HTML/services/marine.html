Marine Diesel

Airylea Motors repair and service fuel systems on all types of marine diesel engines, from small compressors up to 12,000+ HP marine diesels.

We carry an extensive range of injector and pump spares allowing us to carry out repairs quickly and efficiently. Our staff of specialist engineers are based just a stone's throw from Aberdeen harbour, one of the busiest oil industry service ports in the UK.

We are not limited to the Aberdeen area, however. We supply marine fuel injection equipment throughout the UK and abroad, to places as diverse as Brazil, Gibraltar and Nigeria.

Airylea Motors can completely overhaul your fuel injection systems from top to bottom, testing and replacing all components such as plungers, springs, valves and screws. Our Hartridge test benches are of the most modern design and are equal to testing almost every size of fuel injector pump.

Injector Pump Testing On The Hartridge 3000

They allow us to test your fuel system components under artificial running conditions and ensure the delivery of fuel to the makers specifications, increasing your engines performance and reliability.
