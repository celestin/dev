How to download and install the e-books

I have just finished a second release of the ministry of the recovery and Hymns and Spiritual Songs (1962) [English, Deutsche & Dutch... Italiano in progress] for e-readers. Please use the links on this page to buy your Kindle, as this generates a little revenue that helps pay for the hosting for this site!

There are two formats available, supporting all the most common reader devices:

  1. EPUB Suitable for many readers, including Apple's iBooks which works a treat on iPad, iPhone and iPod Touch [ Ministry | Hymns ]
  2. MOBI Specifically for the Amazon Kindle, although it will work on many other devices too [ Ministry | Hymns ]

So you've bought a reader and want to read the ministry on it?

Easy! Follow the simple steps below!

Amazon Kindle

  1. Download the MOBI format ministry (now available MOBI format Hymns too!)
  2. Unzip the file on your Desktop (either Mac or PC) - it should create a single "ministry" folder with subfolders for servants
  3. Attach your Kindle via the supplied USB cable
  4. Drag the ministry folder that you created and drop it onto the documents folder on Kindle drive or device (the ministry has to sit underneath the documents folder)
  5. Wait for the files to copy, then disconnect the Kindle
  6. When it powers up, you should see all the ministry! If not, email Support!

Apple iPad, iPhone or iPod Touch

The great thing about iTunes is that once you have the Books on your computer, you can sync the whole lot to all three of these devices (if you own more than one) and it actually works pretty well on them all.

  1. Download the EPUB format ministry (now available EPUB format Hymns too!)
  2. Unzip the file on your Desktop (either Mac or PC) - it should create a single "ministry" folder with all the epub files inside
  3. Fire up iTunes
  4. Download iBooks
  5. You may have to restart iTunes after installing iBooks
  6. Drag the "ministry" folder onto iTunes (you should see the progress bar at the top as it copies the books)
  7. If you look under "Books" you will see all the ministry, but you won't be able to read it (yet!)
  8. Now attach your first device (e.g. iPad or iPhone) and wait for iTunes to recognise it
  9. When it appears on the left under DEVICES, click on it
  10. Look along the top Summary, Info, Apps... Books! Click on Books
  11. Tick "Sync Books" checkbox and leave "All books" radio button selected
  12. Click the Sync button at the bottom and wait!
  13. After sync'ing, disconnect your device, push the iBooks app and you should see all the ministry! If not, email Support!
  14. (Repeat steps 8 - 13 if you have another Apple device sync'd with iTunes)
