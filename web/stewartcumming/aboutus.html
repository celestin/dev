About Us

Stewart & Cumming are a family run business that have been practising since 1908. Our primary objective is to provide a personal accountancy service to our clients If you would like to arrange an initial free no obligation meeting, please contact us during the hours of 9am - 5pm Monday to Friday

The original company started trading in the Edinburgh City Centre before moving to Shandwick place and then on to Musselburgh at the end of last century


Stewart & Cumming Ltd has a reputation for giving an outstanding service to its clients as well as offering a very personal service which is difficult to find these days
