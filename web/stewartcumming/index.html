Do you need an accountant?

As a start-up business you may have thought you are not big enough to warrant the use of an accountant. If you have come this far without one, that decision has perhaps been justified.

But now you are up and running, the likelihood is you shall need professional advice sooner rather than later. You may be inclined to wait for that time to arise, but there are plenty of reasons why it makes sense to talk to an accountant straight away
